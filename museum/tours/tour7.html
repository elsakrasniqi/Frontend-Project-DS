Italian Baroque Art

Christ Giving the Keys to St. Peter by Guido Reni depicts a pivotal biblical moment when Jesus entrusts St. Peter with the keys to Heaven, symbolizing the establishment of the Church and Peter's leadership role. This Baroque masterpiece showcases Reni's characteristic use of dramatic lighting, soft color tones, and serene composition to emphasize the spiritual significance of the event. The interaction between Christ and Peter reflects humility, grace, and divine authority.
