The Greek Hall

The Greek Hall, known as the Galerie d'Apollon, showcases an extraordinary collection of ancient Greek art, highlighting the rich artistic and cultural heritage of classical Greece. Visitors can marvel at masterpieces such as statues, ceramics, and decorative objects, including iconic works like the Venus de Milo and the Winged Victory of Samothrace. This section emphasizes the development of Greek art through its archaic, classical, and Hellenistic periods, offering insights into the aesthetic and intellectual achievements that influenced Western civilization.
