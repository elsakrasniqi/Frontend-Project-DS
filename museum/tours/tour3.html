The Denon Wing

The Denon Wing is one of the three main wings of the Museum, located on the museum's eastern side. It houses a vast collection of European art, including masterpieces from the Renaissance to the 19th century. The wing is named after Dominique-Vivant Denon, the first director of the Louvre under Napoleon Bonaparte.
